Four-Part Day

Here you can see the start times for the four parts of the day.

You'll be able to edit these in the future.

Morning06:00
Day08:30
Evening19:30
Night22:00

Name your scenes "Morning", "Day", "Evening", or "Night" and the Rooms & Zones control panel will use those scenes for the appropriate time period. It will use a sensible fallback scene if a particular period doesn't have a scene with that name. If there's no appropriate scene, the group will just be turned on with its current settings.
